Student Clubs

There are a wide variety of clubs and enrichment at St. Mary’s Academy for each division. As early as Grade 7, offerings such as Computer Club, Earthsavers Club and Girls on the Run serve budding interests. Junior High School students and Senior High School participate in clubs.

College Counseling

The college counseling process at SMA begins for all students at grade 9. As a values-based, purpose-driven school, St. Mary’s Academy supports and mentors students as they identify their unique qualities, develop their abilities, and set goals.

How do i know the right courses for me?

Create a list of courses to explore and research each course. You can source information on the internet, through online programs and prospectuses offered by the different universities or schools, or even through your family and friends. Take the time to visit college websites to explore not only the subjects included in your course but also to find out the content of each module per subject.

Do i get a Certificate for every course i take?

If you complete all courses in your certificate program, you will earn a certificate that you can share with your professional network. The title of the certificate may vary depending on the institution(s) issuing it. Please check individual certificate pages for the type of certificate issued upon program completion.

Do i get a refund if the course isn't worth my money?

If a product doesn't live up to expectations, if it's not of merchantable quality, then consumers have rights to ask for their money back under the Sale of Goods Act. But what if you are paying for a course that didn't live up to expectations?
